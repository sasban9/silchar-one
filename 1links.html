All Links

1. The Core Public Pages
(Traffic & Conversion)

  • Home Page: The "Hook." Features the FOMO counter, the "Face Brand" slider, and the primary CTA to join.
  • The Directory (The 1000): The main gallery. Searchable by the 100 categories we listed. Each card should feature the "Face" of the brand prominently.
  • Vision & Mission: Houses the Founder’s Letter and the explanation of the "Reflected Visibility" engine.
  • The Treasury (Transparency): A live dashboard showing how much has been collected in fees and exactly where that money is being spent (Marketing, Events, Social Causes).
  • BOS Deals (The Viral Page): A dedicated page for the "Weekly Specials." This is the page members will share most often with their clients.

2. Member-Centric Pages
(The Value Proposition)

  • Individual Profile Pages: Every member gets a custom URL (e.g., bos1000.online/members/chef-rahul). This acts as their "Digital Visiting Card."
  • The Leaderboard: A ranking of members based on client reviews and platform activity. Top members get featured on the home page.
  • Member-to-Member (M2M) Marketplace: A private or public board for the "Barter" system where members offer special rates to each other.
  • Success Stories: Video interviews of members who saw a spike in traffic or business after joining.

3. Utility & Legal Pages
(The Professionalism)

  • Join/Onboarding Form: A multi-step JS form to collect the "Minimum Requirements" (Photos, Bio, Payment).
  • The Waitlist: For when a specific category is full (e.g., "Real Estate is 50/50—Join the waitlist for the next opening").
  • Media Kit: High-res downloads of the BOS1000 logo and "Verified Member" badges for members to use on their own social media.
  • Terms of Membership: The "Code of Conduct" and the rules regarding the seat auctioning process.
  • Contact & Support: A simple way for members to reach the admin for profile updates.

4. Admin & Auth Pages
(The Backend)

  • Member Login/Dashboard: Where the owner logs in to change their "Deal of the Week," upload new photos, and see their page view analytics.
  • Admin Command Center: For you to approve new members, update the Treasury numbers, and manage the "Seat Counter."
