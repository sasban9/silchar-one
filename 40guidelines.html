The TheSilchar1000 Community Guidelines

The "Face-Brand" Identity Rule

  • Real People Only: Your profile must feature a high-quality, professional photograph of the founder or lead service provider.
  • The "Human" Bio: Your business description should be written in the first person. Tell the story of why you do what you do. People buy from people.

The Reciprocity Mandate (The Synergy Loop)

  • Cross-Promotion: Members are encouraged to share the "BOS Deals" page or another member's profile on their social media at least once a month.
  • The "BOS First" Pledge: When you need a service (a lawyer, a plumber, or a caterer), check the TheSilchar1000 directory first. Support the network that supports you.

Content Quality Standards

  • Media Integrity: All photos and videos uploaded must be original. Using stock photos or low-resolution, blurry images is prohibited as it lowers the brand value for the other 999 members.
  • Honest Offers: "BOS Exclusive Deals" must be genuine. Inflating prices only to offer a "discount" is strictly forbidden and can lead to membership suspension.

Review Integrity

  • No "Fake" Praise: Do not buy reviews or use "review farms." We value the authentic voice of Silchar's citizens.
  • Professionalism in Feedback: If a member receives a negative review, they must respond professionally and seek to resolve the issue. We are a club of excellence; we handle complaints with grace.

The "Active Node" Requirement

  • Profile Freshness: To stay high in the search rankings, members should update their "Deal of the Week" or portfolio photos at least once every quarter.
  • Event Participation: While not mandatory, members are strongly encouraged to attend the annual "Silchar Excellence Awards" and networking mixers to strengthen the physical bond of the network.

Enforcement & The "Seat" Policy

Because there are only 1000 seats, they are highly coveted.

  • Warnings: Minor violations of the guidelines will result in a "Yellow Card" (temporary hidden profile).
  • Termination: Repeated violations or unethical business practices will lead to the permanent termination of membership.
  • The Auction Loop: Terminated seats will be immediately moved to the Public Auction tier (starting at ₹1 Lakh+), ensuring that the club always consists of the most committed businesses in the city.

Technical Tip for the JS Expert

To make these guidelines feel "interactive" during onboarding, you can implement a "Guideline Quiz" or an "Interactive Agreement" in your JS form:

  1. Instead of a long "Terms and Conditions" box that nobody reads, use a Step-by-Step Toggle.
  2. The user must click "I Agree" or "I Understand" for each of the 5 pillars before the "Pay & Join" button becomes active.
  3. The "Live" Code Hint: Use localStorage or a state management tool to track their progress through the guidelines so they don't have to restart if they refresh the page.
